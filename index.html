This is an Integrated Student Management System can help the teacher as well as the students of several education institutions. Our main purpose is to provide an all-around environment that can be used by several institutions to stay connected
It provides a plausible atmostphere that can connect the Teachers and their respective Students. Whether it is the matter of keeping a track on the curriculum or on their attendance, this project is build to bring out the most
Salient
Features
The teachers are provided with a functionality to mark attendance and provides notes. Teachers also have the option to assign the homework as well as notify the students regarding some upcoming event or an extra lecture. Teachers can have a thorough information regarding the number of lectures attended by each student and also the option to assign grades
The students in return have the option to submit their assignment and also get the notifications that are sent out by the teachers, in addition to keep a track on their attendance and their grades. They have an access to the notes uploaded by the teachers and upload their own notes
