INTEGRATED STUDENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

SALIENT FEATURES

This is an Integrated Student Management System can help the teacher. as well as the students of several education institutions.

It provides a plausible atmostphere that can connect the Teachers and their respective Students. Whether it is the matter of keeping a track on the curriculum or on their attendance, this project is build to bring out the most

The teachers are provided with a functionality to mark attendance and provides notes. Teachers also have the option to assign the homework as well as notify the students regarding some upcoming event or an extra lecture.

The students in return have the option to submit their assignment and also get the notifications that are sent out by the teachers, in addition to keep a track on their attendance and their grades.
